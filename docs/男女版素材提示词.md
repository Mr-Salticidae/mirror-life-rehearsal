# 男女专属素材提示词包 v2

> 用途：补齐《镜像自我·人生预演》男女两套基础素材。图片用 Midjourney，视频用 Seedance 图生视频。
> 本版不是“把 boy 改成 girl”，而是按不同人生阶段分别锁定发型、衣着、身形轮廓、姿态气质与职业装备。

## 归档命名

图片建议：

- 男生：`app/public/stills/male/{id}.jpg`
- 女生：`app/public/stills/female/{id}.jpg`

视频建议：

- 男生：`app/public/videos/male/{id}.mp4`
- 女生：`app/public/videos/female/{id}.mp4`

第一批只做 13 个基础动态节点：`node_a`、`node_b`、`node_f`、`node_c`、`node_g`、`node_d`、`node_h`、`node_e`、`career_soldier`、`career_painter`、`career_racer`、`career_musician`、`career_astronaut`。

## Midjourney 总规则

所有图必须保持电影剧照，不要做头像、写真、海报立绘。主角尽量背影、侧背影、剪影，不露正脸。

通用参数：

```text
cinematic film still, anamorphic widescreen, 35mm film grain, volumetric light, deep shadows, shallow depth of field, restrained composition, clear visual hierarchy, foreground-midground-background layering --ar 16:9 --v 8.1 --style raw --preview --no text, watermark, logo, blurry, low quality, front-facing face, selfie, portrait studio, beauty shot
```

## 男生版角色锚

- 7 岁：Chinese boy, short slightly messy black hair, worn white T-shirt, khaki shorts, scuffed canvas shoes, small narrow shoulders, curious but quiet body language
- 17 岁：Chinese teenage boy, cropped black hair, blue-white school tracksuit jacket, dark school pants, black backpack, lean shoulders, hesitant but stubborn posture
- 22 岁：young Chinese man, simple dark hoodie over white T-shirt, worn jeans, cheap canvas sneakers, tired shoulders, first-salary restraint
- 25 岁：young Chinese man, black short hair, dark coat, clean but modest outfit, standing still with a tense straight back
- 26 岁职业：按职业装备锁定，但仍保持同一个男生长大后的背影轮廓：short black hair, lean adult build, calm controlled posture

## 女生版角色锚

- 7 岁：Chinese girl, chin-length black bob with a small red hair clip, worn white T-shirt, navy shorts or skirt, scuffed canvas shoes, small narrow shoulders, curious but quiet body language
- 17 岁：Chinese teenage girl, low ponytail or chin-length bob, blue-white school tracksuit jacket, dark school skirt or pants, canvas tote bag, hesitant but stubborn posture
- 22 岁：young Chinese woman, shoulder-length black hair tied loosely, cream cardigan over plain T-shirt, worn jeans, cheap canvas sneakers, tired shoulders, first-salary restraint
- 25 岁：young Chinese woman, shoulder-length black hair, dark long coat, clean but modest outfit, standing still with a tense straight back
- 26 岁职业：按职业装备锁定，但仍保持同一个女生长大后的背影轮廓：tied black hair or tucked hair, lean adult build, calm controlled posture
